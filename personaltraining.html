OUR FITNESS COACHES

David Cooper - Strength and Conditioning Expert

David Cooper is a seasoned fitness coach with over 10 years of experience in the fitness industry. He is a certified personal trainer specializing in strength training, cardiovascular fitness, and functional movements. David's training philosophy focuses on building strength, endurance, and flexibility through a mix of traditional weight training, high-intensity interval training (HIIT), and functional exercises. He emphasizes proper form, injury prevention, and creating customized programs tailored to each client's fitness goals. Whether you're training for a specific event, trying to lose weight, or simply improving your overall fitness, David’s workouts will push you to reach new heights. David believes that a strong foundation in strength training is key to overall physical health. He helps clients progressively increase their strength while maintaining functional movement patterns, making everyday activities easier and more enjoyable.

Isabela Manchester - Yoga and Flexibility Specialist

Isabela Manchester is an expert yoga instructor with a deep passion for Hatha and Vinyasa yoga. With over 8 years of teaching experience, she has helped countless individuals improve their flexibility, strength, and mental well-being through yoga practice. Isabela’s classes combine mindful breathing, gentle stretches, and strength-building postures to enhance both physical and mental health. She specializes in making yoga accessible to all levels, whether you're a beginner looking to improve flexibility or an advanced practitioner aiming to deepen your practice. Isabela’s teaching focuses on proper alignment, balance, and breath control, ensuring a calming environment that promotes mental clarity and emotional well-being. Her classes are designed to reduce stress, increase flexibility, and create a sense of peace both in and out of the studio.

Caleb Wilder - HIIT and Functional Fitness Specialist

Caleb Wilder is an experienced fitness coach who specializes in high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and functional conditioning. His training methods are designed to challenge clients' endurance, strength, and agility, helping them develop the fitness needed for everyday life and competitive sports. Caleb’s approach to training combines short bursts of intense exercise with periods of rest to help clients burn fat, build muscle, and improve overall fitness levels. His workouts are dynamic, targeting all major muscle groups, and are designed to push clients to their limits. Caleb emphasizes functional movements that improve everyday mobility, such as squats, lunges, and core work. His philosophy is based on the idea that fitness should not only look good but should improve the way you feel and move in your daily life.
