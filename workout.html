Our Workout Plans

Whether you're a beginner or advanced, we have tailored workout routines to help you reach your fitness goals.

Beginner Full-Body Workout

Duration: 30-45 minutes | Frequency: 3 days a week

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes light cardio
  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets, hold for 20-30 seconds
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
  • Cool-down: Stretching

Intermediate Strength Training

Duration: 45-60 minutes | Frequency: 4 days a week

Day 1: Upper Body

  • Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Pull-ups: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 2: Lower Body

  • Barbell Squats: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps

Healthy Recipes

Grilled Chicken & Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken breast (grilled)
  • 1/2 cup quinoa (cooked)
  • Mixed greens (spinach, kale, arugula)
  • 1/2 avocado
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber slices
  • Olive oil and lemon for dressing

    • Instructions:

    1. Grill the chicken breast and slice it thinly.
    2. Cook quinoa as per package instructions.
    3. Toss the quinoa with the greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and avocado.
    4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, and top with grilled chicken slices.

Sweet Potato & Black Bean Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 large sweet potato (diced)
  • 1 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot, sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add the diced sweet potato, chili powder, and cumin. Stir for a minute.
  3. Pour in the diced tomatoes and black beans. Bring to a simmer and cook for 25 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender.
  4. Serve warm with some avocado or Greek yogurt on top.

Protein-Packed Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 scoop of protein powder (whey or plant-based)
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter or almond butter
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 1/2 cup spinach (optional for added greens)

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and enjoy as a post-workout refuel.

Tuna & Avocado Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 can tuna in water (drained)
  • 1/2 avocado (mashed)
  • Mixed greens (like arugula, spinach)
  • Red onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the drained tuna with mashed avocado.
  2. Toss the mixed greens and red onion in a separate bowl.
  3. Combine the two, then drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper and enjoy!
